About Gorilla Business Group

Gorilla Business Group is a South African family-owned industrial and construction venture focused on resilient, locally-made concrete products and practical construction services. Founded with the aim of improving housing delivery and infrastructure resilience in peri-urban communities, Gorilla Bricks combines modern manufacturing with hands-on construction know-how.

Our mission

To manufacture and supply reliable, cost-effective concrete products and provide construction services that meet SANS standards, support local employment, and deliver value to homeowners, contractors and municipalities across Gauteng and Mpumalanga.

Our values

  • Quality: SANS-compliant manufacturing and consistent site testing.
  • Local impact: Job creation, skills transfer and ethical procurement.
  • Reliability: On-time deliveries, clear lead times and transparent pricing.
  • Safety & Compliance: Environmental awareness and health & safety practices on site.

Who we serve

Homeowners renovating or building new homes, small and medium developers, landscaping contractors, municipalities pursuing road/kerb projects, and commercial clients requiring bulk deliveries.

Our Services

Comprehensive offering tailored for South African residential, commercial and municipal projects. We price competitively and support delivery or turnkey installation.

Stock Bricks

Traditional face bricks for walls, boundary walls, and domestic housing.

Available in multiple colours and strengths. Manufactured to resist local climatic variation and designed for ease of laying.

Block Bricks (Hollow & Solid)

Structural and non-structural block solutions for foundations and retaining walls.

High compressive strength options, suitable for load-bearing walls and civil applications.

Pavers & Interlocking Blocks

Driveways, patios, walkways and commercial yards.

Multiple colours and patterns, slip-resistant finishes, and long-life performance under South African sun and seasonal rains.

Kerbs & Channels

Road edge kerbs, edging, and drainage channels for municipal and private roads.

Manufactured to SANS tolerances and available in standard and custom profiles.

Concrete Columns & Custom Precast

Structural columns, lintels and decorative precast elements.

Custom sizes and reinforcement options — suitable for housing and light commercial frames.

Delivery & Logistics

Site delivery across Gauteng & Mpumalanga.

We coordinate truck loading, offloading and short-distance haulage with clear lead times and tracking for bulk orders.

Turnkey Installation & Masonry

From site preparation and foundations to bricklaying and finishes.

Our experienced teams deliver small-to-medium residential builds and landscaping installations on a contract basis.

Technical Support & Testing

Quality assurance testing, material specs, and technical drawings.

On-request cube-testing, compressive strength tests and SANS-compliant certificates for tender submissions.
